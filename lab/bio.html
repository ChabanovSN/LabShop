Описание:

The quad fin spin-off on the classic Kookumber shape, makes this board more responsive and able to be pushed a little more aggressively. suitable for a variety of waves.

BEST FOR: Getting into waves and maintaining speed; without sacrificing the ability to hit the lip. This board really flows off the tail.

FIN: Future boxes or glass on wooden fins, by Gully, are standard on the Quadkumber.
